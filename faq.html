Pricing & Billing

Is there a free trial?
+
Yes — all plans come with a 14-day free trial with full Professional plan access. No credit card required to start.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), ACH bank transfers, and wire transfers for annual contracts over $10,000.
Can I change plans mid-cycle?
+
Upgrades are prorated and take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts?
+
Yes — 50% off for qualifying nonprofits and startups (under 3 years, under $2M raised). Contact sales@stacklyai.com with documentation.
What happens if I exceed my token limit?
+
You'll receive email alerts at 80% and 95% usage. After the limit, you can upgrade, purchase a top-up, or wait for the monthly reset. We never cut off active conversations mid-session.

Getting Started

How do I get started with Stackly AI?
+
Sign up at stacklyai.com/register, choose your plan (or start free), and follow our onboarding wizard. Most customers have their first AI workflow live within an hour.
Do I need coding or ML experience?
+
Not at all. Stackly AI is designed for business users and developers alike. Our visual workflow builder requires zero code. For advanced customization, we provide a full REST API and Python SDK.
How long does onboarding take?
+
Self-serve customers can be up and running in under an hour. Enterprise onboarding with dedicated support typically takes 5–10 business days including integrations and team training.

Security & Privacy

Is my data used to train your models?
+
No. Customer data is never used to train our shared models. Your data stays in your isolated environment. We provide a data processing agreement (DPA) confirming this.
What compliance certifications do you hold?
+
We are SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified. We are also HIPAA-eligible and GDPR compliant. Full compliance documentation is available on request.
Where is my data stored?
+
Data is stored in AWS US-East-1 by default. Enterprise customers can choose EU (Frankfurt) or APAC (Singapore) regions, or opt for on-premises deployment.

Technical

What integrations do you support?
+
We offer 500+ native integrations including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Snowflake, BigQuery, Stripe, Jira, Zendesk, and more. Custom integrations can be built via our REST API and webhooks.
What is your uptime SLA?
+
We guarantee 99.9% uptime for Professional plans and 99.99% for Enterprise. Historical uptime is published on our status page at status.stacklyai.com.
Can I use my own AI models?
+
Yes. Stackly AI supports bring-your-own-model (BYOM) for Enterprise customers. You can deploy custom fine-tuned models or connect to any OpenAI-compatible API endpoint.
