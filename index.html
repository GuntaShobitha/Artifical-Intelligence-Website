FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I deploy my first AI model?
+
Most users deploy their first production model within 48 hours of signing up. Our guided setup wizard walks you through data connection, model selection, and workflow configuration step by step. No machine learning experience required.
What data does Stackly AI have access to?
+
Stackly AI only accesses data you explicitly connect. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, never used to train shared models, and fully owned by you. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant.
Can I use my own LLM or fine-tuned model?
+
Yes. You can bring your own models from Hugging Face, Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, or any custom API endpoint. Stackly AI also supports fine-tuning on Professional and Enterprise plans.
What happens when I exceed my token limit?
+
You'll receive an email notification at 80% and 95% usage. After hitting your limit, you can either upgrade your plan, purchase a token top-up, or wait for the monthly reset. We never cut off service mid-conversation.
Is there a free trial available?
+
Yes — all plans come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You get full access to Professional plan features so you can evaluate the platform with real workloads before committing.
Do you offer discounts for startups and nonprofits?
+
Yes. We offer 50% off on all plans for qualifying startups (under 3 years old, under $2M raised) and nonprofits. Contact our sales team with documentation and we'll apply the discount within 24 hours.
