Our Story

Building AI that Actually Works

Stackly AI was founded with one belief: enterprise AI should be powerful, transparent, and accessible to every team — not just the ones with PhDs.

Our Origin

From a Hackathon to Enterprise Scale

Stackly AI started in 2021 as a weekend hackathon project by three engineers frustrated with how difficult it was to deploy AI in production. What began as a 72-hour sprint became a platform trusted by 50,000+ users across 40+ countries.

Today, Stackly AI offers the most complete AI platform for teams — combining generative AI, machine learning automation, computer vision, and analytics into a single unified workspace. No duct tape required.
